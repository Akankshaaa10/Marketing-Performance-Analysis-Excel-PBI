# Marketing Performance Analysis (Excel & Power BI)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using Excel and Power BI. The focus is on evaluating campaign efficiency, revenue generation, customer conversion behavior, and marketing ROI across different channels and campaign types.

The analysis helps identify:
- High-performing marketing channels
- Revenue and ad spend trends
- Customer conversion patterns
- Campaign effectiveness and profitability

The goal of this project is to help marketing teams:
- Improve campaign performance
- Optimize marketing spend
- Increase conversions and ROI
- Track revenue growth across channels

---

## Problem Statement

Marketing teams run campaigns across multiple platforms such as Google Ads, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Email Marketing. Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to measure campaign effectiveness, monitor ROI, and identify high-performing channels.

This project answers the following questions:

- Which marketing channels generate the highest revenue?
- Which campaign types drive the most conversions?
- How does revenue trend over time?
- What is the overall marketing ROI and ROAS?
- Where do customers drop off in the marketing funnel?
- Which campaigns perform best based on revenue and ROI?

---

- Excel – Data preparation and validation
- Power BI – Data modeling, DAX measures, and dashboard development

## Key Analysis Steps

### 1. Data Preparation (Excel)

- Validated campaign performance metrics
- Checked for duplicates and missing values
- Verified KPI calculations and business logic
- Ensured proper formatting for dates and numerical columns

---

### 2. Data Modeling (Power BI)

- Imported Excel dataset into Power BI
- Verified data types and formatting
- Built dashboard-ready reporting structure

---

### 3. DAX Measures

Key metrics created using DAX:
- Total Revenue
- Total Ad Spend
- Total Conversions
- Average ROI
- Average ROAS

---

### 4. Marketing Funnel Analysis

A marketing funnel was created to track customer progression:

Impressions → Clicks → Conversions

This helps visualize customer drop-off and campaign effectiveness.

---

## Dashboard Metrics

The dashboard includes:
- Total Revenue
- Total Ad Spend
- Total Conversions
- Average ROI
- Average ROAS

---

## Dashboard Insights

Key insights from the analysis:

- Google Ads generated the highest revenue among all marketing channels.
- Email Marketing achieved strong ROI with relatively low ad spend.
- Revenue showed consistent monthly fluctuations across campaigns.
- Awareness and Conversion campaigns represented a major share of campaign activity.
- Significant drop-off was observed between impressions and conversions in the marketing funnel.

---

## Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- Increase investment in high-performing marketing channels.
- Optimize campaigns with lower conversion efficiency.
- Improve targeting strategies to reduce funnel drop-off.
- Focus on campaigns generating high ROI and strong revenue growth.
- Continuously monitor campaign performance trends for better budget allocation.
